Directions

The 2026 D.I.V.E. Forum will be held at Zhiyuan Swimming Gymnasium, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Minhang Campus, Shanghai, China. The venue is located inside the SJTU campus and will host the forum activities, exhibitions, and competition-related events.

You may use the map below to navigate to the venue. We recommend that visitors enter from a nearby campus gate and follow on-site signs to Zhiyuan Swimming Gymnasium. More detailed arrival information will be provided closer to the event.
