About D.I.V.E Forum

The 2026 D.I.V.E. Forum, short for Discover Intelligence Via Engineering, is a comprehensive robotics forum hosted by the School of Automation and Intelligent Sensing at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. The forum is designed for young students, university participants, and researchers, and brings together science outreach, academic exchange, engineering practice, competition, and industrial application in one event.

The forum focuses on exploring intelligence through engineering practice. It covers a wide range of robotic topics, from software to hardware, and from underwater systems to embodied and general-purpose robotics. The event combines physical exhibitions, interactive demonstrations, engineering competitions, public science talks, and academic forums, together with online livestreaming through new media platforms.

The 2026 D.I.V.E. Forum will be held together with the 2026 MATE International Underwater Robotics Competition China Regional Event. Outstanding teams in the regional competition will have the opportunity to advance to the world championship. The forum will also collaborate with the 2026 WBCD Bimanual Robotics Challenge, presenting real robotic platforms and encouraging on-site technical exchange, system debugging, and project demonstration.
